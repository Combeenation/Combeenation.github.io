User Permissions

Roles per company

Every user can be assigned to multiple companies with it's own individual role.
The roles always include the permissions of the preceding one.

Basic explanation

Role Info
Guest Read-only access to the backend.
E.g. to view the metrics or latest changes of configurators.
Collaborator Can create/edit/delete drafts but isn't allowed to publish them.
Developer Similar to Collaborator and can also publish drafts.
Manager Is allowed to edit the staff (invite, manage permissions) and withdraw configurators.
Admin Can manage the configurators and whitelist.
This role is only given upon special agreement with Combeenation.

Manage Staff

Only Manager or Admin

The Staff tab can be found in the company settings (gear-icon next to the company name). In this view you can do the following:

  • Invite new users
  • Edit role of existing users
  • Revoke access to the company
